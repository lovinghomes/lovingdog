Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting customer data.

Last updated: March 2024

1. Introduction

Loving Homes ("the Company", "we", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following information:

  • Contact details (name, email, phone number)
  • Dog information (name, breed/type, age, health condition)
  • Booking and payment information
  • Visit and service records
  • Photos and videos of your dog (with your consent)

3. How We Use Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide requested services
  • Contact you regarding bookings and services
  • Improve the quality of our services
  • Comply with laws and regulations
  • Send updates and promotions (with your consent)

4. Data Protection

We use advanced security measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Data encryption
  • Firewalls
  • Access control
  • Regular backups

5. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties except in these cases:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To provide services (e.g., payment providers)
  • When legally required

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request data deletion
  • Object to data processing
  • Request a copy of your data

7. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to improve your experience. You can control cookies via your browser settings.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of major changes via email or website notice.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:
