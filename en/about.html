About

Loving Homes is a luxury dog hotel in Hong Kong specialized in premium pet care services.

Our Story

Loving Homes was founded to provide a safe and luxurious home for dogs while their owners are away. We believe your dog deserves exceptional comfort and care.

With years of experience and dedication, we have become a trusted choice for many dog owners in Hong Kong.
